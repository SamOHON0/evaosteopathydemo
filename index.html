Treatments

Five disciplines. One body in mind.

Different bodies need different approaches. We draw from a deep toolkit and tailor every session to what your body is asking for.

The Foundation

Osteopathy

An independent, holistic method focused on recognising and treating functional disorders of the body. Gentle hands-on techniques that restore movement, ease tension, and let the body return to balance.

From birth onwards

Pediatric Osteopathy

Specialist gentle treatment for infants and children. Often helpful with colic, reflux, sleep difficulties, and tension carried from birth. The lightest of touches, designed for the smallest patients.

Movement & Rehab

Physiotherapy

Active rehabilitation, manual therapy, exercise prescription. Often combined with osteopathy in the same session for a complete approach to musculoskeletal issues, recovery, and injury prevention.

Specialist technique

Atlas Therapy

The atlas is the topmost vertebra and an often-overlooked source of issues from headaches to balance problems. A certified, gentle correction technique that can have a profound effect on the rest of the body.

Fascia work

Fascial Distortion Model

A modern, certified approach that reads the body's pain through the patient's own gestures and addresses six specific fascial distortions. Often delivers fast results for acute musculoskeletal pain.

Empowerment

Self-Healing Technique

SHT teaches you simple, powerful methods to take home. Tools for managing pain, releasing tension, and supporting your own recovery between sessions. The body is its own best healer.

Conditions treated

If your body's telling you something is off, we can probably help.

Neck & Back Pain

Acute or chronic. From posture to old injuries to disc-related issues.

Migraines & Headaches

ADHD Tension

Supporting children with ADHD-related body tension and dysregulation.

Reflux

Particularly common in newborns. Gentle techniques to ease the diaphragm.

Infant Colic

Postural Issues

Sports Injuries

Acute and recurring. FDM in particular is excellent for fast resolution.

Pregnancy-Related

Pelvic, sacrum, and lower back changes during and after pregnancy.

Every session is tailored to the person on the table. Here's the rhythm a first visit usually follows.

i

Listening first

The session begins with a careful conversation. Your history, what brought you in, where the body feels off. The detail matters.

ii

Hands-on assessment

A gentle, full-body assessment to find the functional disorders that may be behind the symptoms, often somewhere quite different from where it hurts.

iii

The treatment

Drawing from osteopathy, physio, atlas therapy and FDM as the body asks. Mostly gentle. Manipulation only when needed and only with consent.

iv

Tools to take home

Self-healing techniques, exercises, posture cues. Practical things you can do at home to support the work between sessions.

Before your first session.

Osteopathy is an independent, holistic method of therapy focused on the recognition and individual treatment of functional disorders of the body. Treatment is typically gentle and works with the body's natural ability to heal.

Treatment is usually quite gentle. Manipulation techniques are used when appropriate, but in many cases they are not needed at all. Your comfort and your body's response guide the approach, and nothing happens without your consent.

Neck and back pain, coccyx pain, migraines and headaches, sleep difficulties, reflux, colic, ADHD-related tension, postural issues, pregnancy-related changes, and many other functional conditions in adults, children, and infants. If you're not sure whether we can help, please ask.
